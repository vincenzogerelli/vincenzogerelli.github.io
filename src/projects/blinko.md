Blinko is designed for early learners during their prime language-learning window. Children receive missions to find everyday objects using the built-in camera, powered by CoreML for real-time object recognition.

Once found, objects transform into illustrated cards, and mini-games reinforce vocabulary through repetition and active recall. Supports five languages: English, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, and Japanese.

Selected among the top 4 projects of the Apple Developer Academy and presented to 500+ attendees including Apple engineers.

**Tech stack:** Swift, SwiftUI, CoreML, Machine Learning.
